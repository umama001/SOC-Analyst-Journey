# Day 5 – Suspicious Domain Investigation

## Alert Description
A DNS request was made to the domain free-gift-card123.xyz

## Initial Analysis
The domain looks suspicious because it uses words like “free” and “gift card,” which are commonly used in scams and phishing attacks. Legitimate companies usually use clean and professional domain names.

## Possible Threat
This domain could be used for phishing, scams, or malware delivery. It may trick users into clicking links, entering personal information, or downloading malicious files.

## Decision
Escalate

## Reason
The domain shows signs of social engineering and could pose a risk to users. Escalating ensures further investigation to prevent potential threats.
