# Day 4 – Network Traffic Patterns

## Activity Observed
I opened Wikipedia, Amazon, and TryHackMe in my browser. I also did a quick Google search and watched a short video on YouTube.

## Traffic Patterns
Most of the traffic I saw was DNS queries, TLS/HTTPS packets, and TCP packets. Browsing the websites triggered DNS queries, and watching the video caused lots of TCP packets.

## Interesting Observation
noticed multiple DNS queries to Wikipedia, Amazon, and TryHackMe. TLS traffic was frequent while loading the websites and streaming the YouTube video.

## Suspicious or Normal?
Everything looked normal because the traffic was generated by my own browsing, searches, and video streaming. Nothing unusual was observed.
